A some what larger patch (some light argue "feature") this time around that focused on standardizing the layout and styling of the cards that are used to navigate between elements in the application. All cards in the novel structure now feature Edit, Archive and Delete buttons. The cards in the planning modules (CHaracters, Locations and Items) just have an Edit button for now. The Archive and Delete features are not included in phase 1. Finally you can now drag-and-drop reorder all cards on all boards.

This work has also fixed two other issues that arose during testing; the ability to archive a scene and delete a scene. The functionality to perform these tasks was already in place, the only thing missing was the buttons to trigger the actions. Version 0.3.1 implemented those buttons on all cards, including scene cards, and once that was done the archive and delete features for scenes became available.

Next? Bug fixes.
