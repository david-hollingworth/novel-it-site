Novel-It is the product of my search for an open-source solution for writing and planning novels. When I couldn't find one that fit my needs, 
I decided to create it myself. It's designed to be simple, flexible, and easy to use. I hope it helps other writers in their creative process.

<!--more-->

The purpose of this blog is to provide updates on the development of Novel-It for a non-technical audience. I'll share insights into the features I'm 
working on, the challenges I encounter, and the progress I make. If you're interested in following along with the development of Novel-It, 
this is the place to be.

If you'd like to try Novel-It yourself, you can find the source code on GitHub: 
<a href="https://github.com/david-hollingworth/novel-it" class="hover:opacity-70">https://github.com/david-hollingworth/novel-it</a>

<i>Happy writing</i>
